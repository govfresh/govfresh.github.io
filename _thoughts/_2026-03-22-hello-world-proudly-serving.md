After several years and [25 chapters](https://proudlyservingbook.com/contents/) from more than [20 contributors](https://proudlyservingbook.com/contributors/), I’m extremely proud to announce that 
"[Proudly Serving: Public service with and for the people](https://proudlyservingbook.com/)" has launched.

"Proudly Serving" is available in [multiple formats](https://proudlyservingbook.com/book/), including print (at cost) and free on Amazon Kindle, Google Play Books, Apple Books and web, HTML, EPUB and PDF formats.

It’s been an honor to make this with so many amazing people committed to public service and a better way of doing civics. The patience, persistence and commitment from everyone involved, and the final product, will forever inspire me.

Much love to everyone who made this possible.

[Get Proudly Serving.](https://proudlyservingbook.com/)
